Human Reflex Arc

Interactive 3D simulation of an involuntary neural pathway response.

1. Receptor

Skin receptors detect harmful stimulus (thorn) and generate pain signal.

2. Sensory Neuron

Electrical impulse travels along the afferent nerve to the spinal cord.

3. Interneuron

Signal transfers within gray matter, bridging sensory and motor neurons.

4. Motor Neuron

Action potential travels via efferent nerve to the local muscle.

5. Effector

Bicep muscle contracts, physically withdrawing the hand from danger.
